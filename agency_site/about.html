ABOUT


Xtendedmusic Agency Ltd. delivers you the best artists to your event or festival and provides services in event production, promotion and management.

Xtendedmusic Agency Ltd. is a company founded by Mr. Sami Vepsäläinen who is also one of the founding members behind Weekend Festival brand and has worked with hundreds of live events, artists bookings and concert productions in various roles. From Lil Wayne to Jennifer Lopez, from Paul Anka to David Guetta and Pitbull – good music has no boundaries.

Xtendedmusic Agency offers booking and production services to festivals and live entertainment promoters in Finland, Baltics and abroad. We have strong connections with other international talent agencies from Europe to US. We also offer management services to arists like The Second Level, KIRO and build collaboration with various artists and their management agencies around the globe.

Being successful is a marriage of many things. We believe in long term business relationships and want to help our clients and our talent to reach their goals. Live music industry has a tendency to overcomplicate things and we are out on a mission to make things a little more simple, efficient and hassle free. If you require professional talent booking service, marketing services or production help to your festival, event or concert do not hesitate to contact us.
