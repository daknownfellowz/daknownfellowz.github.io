ABOUT

Xtendedmusic Agency Ltd. delivers you the best artists to your event or festival and provides services in event production, promotion and management.

Xtendedmusic Agency Ltd. is a company based in Helsinki, Finland. The CEO & founder Mr. Sami Vepsäläinen is also one of the founding members of Weekend Festival and has worked with hundreds of live events, artists bookings and concert productions in various roles in the past 10 years from Lil Wayne to Jennifer Lopez, from Paul Anka to David Guetta and Green Day. Xtendedmusic Agency offers booking and production services to festivals and live entertainment promoters in Finland, Baltics and abroad. We have strong connections with other international talent agencies in Europe and US. All though our focus is strong on electronic music artists and DJ's we work with all music genres - good entertainment has no boundaries. 

We are well experienced in collaborating with various brands and connecting them with talent, live music events and festivals. Our references include Huawei, Samsung, Genelec and Tesla just to name a few. We can connect your brand with artists and events directly and efficiently. In case you are a brand representative looking for an innovative way to get your message trough to your customers - we can offer plenty of solutions for content creation and marketing. Xtendedmusic Agency also provides management services for artists like The Second Level and KIRO and we are looking forward to expanding the family in the future. 

We believe in long term business relationships and want to help our clients and talent to reach their goals. Music and entertainment industry can sometimes seem complicated and we are out on a mission to make things a little more simple, efficient and hassle free. If you require a professional talent booking service, marketing services, collaboration with your product, brand or service, production help for your event, concert or festival we are ready to serve.
