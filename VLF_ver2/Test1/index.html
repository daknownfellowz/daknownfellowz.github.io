ALORA & SENII

DJ’S FROM MARS

Biography

The Djs From Mars bring the mashup attitude into electrohouse. They started remixing big pop hits for their gigs in 2004 just for the fun of it. In a couple of years they became recognized as one of the biggest names in the mashup scene. Their videos on Youtube get over 10 millions of views, and the hype is still growing.

DJ's From Mars bootlegs we're soon noticed by labels all around the world, and they started working on remixes for big pop and dance acts such as Bimbo Jones, Sean Paul, Pitbull, Yves La Rock, Sophie Ellis Bextor, Fragma, Ciara Cascada, Coolio, Ennio Morricone, and many more. In the US they started working with Jason Nevins, who brought them some big major act remixes.

DJ's From Mars's tracks have been playlisted and supported by many top djs like David Guetta, Carl Cox, Bob Sinclar and many more. These two aliens have toured all around the world, their cartoon box head show has been seen inGermany, Austria, France, Sweden, Italy, Australia, USA, Portugal, Spain, Poland, Brazil etc.

Their goal - simply to conquer the world - so stay tuned and be aware. ;)

The follow up remix of 'Don't be So Shy' also got the platinum status and a strong international radio support. In SUMMER 2016 the track hit Top 10 Shazam worldwide. The Remix became number 1 hit in Russia, Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Germany, Austria, Australia, France, Poland, Greece, Bulgaria, Romania, Ukraine, Georgia, Turkey, Kazakhstan and many more countries. Three following tunes 'Filatov & Karas - Satellite', 'Filatov & Karas - Tell it to my heart' and 'Eric Saade feat Filatov & Karas, Gustaf Noren - Wide Awake' already started rocking national charts in Europe.

SAK NOEL

Biography

1983 - Sak Noel is born in La Cellera de Ter (Girona), Catalonia. During his early ages, he develops a huge interest for artistic creation. After playing with different disciplines, such as writing, painting or filmmaking, he ends up choosing music as the main interest.

2003 - Sak Noel quits college to start an entertainment company called 'Moguda' ('Movement'). In this company, Sak Noel explores all his interests working as music producer, journalist, video director and TV presenter, among others.

2010 - After working several years on 'Moguda' project, Sak Noel produces his first big hit 'Loca People'. He closes 'Moguda' and creates his own company 'Noel Music'.

2011 - 'Loca People' becomes an international sensation being number 1 in more than 15 countries around the globe. Sak Noel is the 1st Catalan artist to be number 1 on the BBC Official Singles Chart in UK. The song gets over 300 million views on Youtube and is licensed by labels like Ultra Records, Spinnin' Records or Sony Music. It also achieves Golden Disc in Germany, Holland, Italy and Denmark. Later that year, Sak releases the 'Loca People' follow up: 'Paso'.

2012 - Sak Noel is nominated for 'Best Urban Song' at the Latin Grammys for Pitbull's remix of 'Loca People'. He also wins in the 'Best Latin Dance Track' category at the International Dance Music Awards during the Miami Winter Conference. Later that year, Sak Noel releases the 3rd single, 'Where', finishing his 1st EP called 'Crazy Society Triolgy' which includes 'Loca People', 'Paso' and 'Where'.

2013 - Sak Noel releases 'Party On My Level' with Miami singer Sito Rocks and 'I Am The Law'.

2014 - Sak Noel releases 'Young & Reckless' featuring Grammy winners Da Beat Freakz. He is the 1st Catalan Dj ever to perform at the Official Latin Grammys After Party at Hakkasan, in Las Vegas.

2015 - Sak Noel releases 'No Boyfriend' featuring Mayra Veronica. Song debuts as number 40 on the US Dance Billboard Chart and peaks 32 as the most played song on US Radio. 'No Boyfriend' gets over 20 million views on Youtube and is released by Ultra Records. Later that year, he releases 'Pinga' which gets nominated as Best Latin Dance Track at International Dance Music Awards during the Miami Winter Conference. By the end of the year, Sak Noel releases his 1st album: 'Born To Party'. His Youtube channel breaks the 100k subscribers barrier and receives the Official Youtube certification.

2016 - Sak Noel released 'Trumpets' featuring international star Sean Paul on his own label Barnaton. The track became a massive global hit and 2017 the journey continues and Sak Noel has been confirmed to play big festival and club shows in the summer of 2017.

THE SECOND LEVEL

Biography

The Second Level (Daniel Helenius) is a young DJ / producer from Helsinki, Finland. His music combines energetic future house, Melbourne bounce and various other electronic music influenes with a modern pop twist. His live career started with a bang as he won a dj competition in Helsinki 2014 and was discovered by Weekend Festival's head promoter who decided to give him a chance to play an opening slot for David Guetta in Estonia. The show was a success and he got a chance to perform right before Guetta for a crowd of 15.000 people. Since then there has been little doubt that The Second Level is at home both on big festival stages as well as playing more intimate club venues.

At Weekend Festival 2015 The Second Level played a closing finale for 30.000 people right after Avicii had left the stage and was also featured in the Weekend Festival's 2015 after movie. In 2016 he played several festival and club shows and did his personal records playing 4 shows within 36 hours during Weekend Festival in Finland and Estonia. In addition to great live shows he put lots of focus in producing his own solo tracks and collaborated with different artists and producers such as Jaakko Salovaara and Lenno, both internationally accomplished Finnish music producers.

The Second Level is definitely one of the most potential independent DJ/producers in Finland at the moment. His first independent single 'Monday feat. Lola Loog' has gained over 300.000 streams in Spotify and it's video features him playing on various venues from big festival stages to the biggest clubs in Finland and Estonia. In less than three years The Second Level has already made an impact on the dance music scene in Finland and Estonia and he is setting his sights to new regions in 2017. At the moment The Second Level is getting ready to release his new music and preparing for a busy summer tour playing various clubs and festivals.

VIGILAND

Biography

From the soundtrack to the party to one of Sweden's hottest viral exports, Vigiland continue to make scoring back-to-back hit records look funs. Walking a fine line between the peak of commercial dance music and the more untamed Melbourne bounce sound that first gave the duo rise, the past several years have seen Vigiland become a consistent source of infectious viral hits, garnering millions of streams and multi-platinum certification along the way.

Joining Universal Music Sweden in 2015, initial hit singles 'UFO,' 'Shots & Squats' and 'Pong Dance' garnered the duo more than 250 million streams collectively, setting the Swedish Grammis nominated duo up for a marker summer of touring and national festival appearances throughout. Sophomore single 'Let's Escape' showcased a fresh strand of the duo's anthemic electronic pop sound for 2016, pairing that fierce energy with infectious singalong moments for an early Fall smash from the multi-platinum certified act.

Vigiland's successes for 2016 go far beyond that of their studio output. Debuting at the Latin MTV VMA's in the Summer and surpassing more than 300 million total streams, all signs pointed to a huge ascent for the boys and their boundary breaking approach to the electronic dance world. With a string of exciting musical and live announcements still to come for 2017, the tongue in cheek movements of this trailblazing duo continue to roll to the tune of consecutive hits from the breakthrough act.
