THE SECOND LEVEL

Biography


The Second Level is a young DJ / producer from Helsinki, Finland. His musical style combines energetic future house, Melbourne bounce and various other electronic dance music genres. He started his live show career with a bang as he won a dj competition in 2014 and had a chance to play as opening act for David Guetta in Tallinn for 15.000 people.

At Weekend Festival 2015 The Second Level played a closing finale for 30.000 people and he was also featured in the Weekend Festival’s 2015 after movie. In 2016 The Second Level played festival and club shows including 4 shows in 36 hours during Weekend Festival in Finland and Baltic. Currently The Second Level is working on new music with top Finnish producers and building international collaboration for 2017.

The Second Level is definitely the most potential independent DJ/producer in Finland at the moment. In addition of having the support of the Finnish dance music scene and Northern Europe’s biggest EDM festival he has already made an impact on the dance music scene in Finland and Estonia.

KIRO

Biography


KIRO is one of the most interesting upcoming DJ/producers from Finland. Also known as a platinum-selling pop producer in Finland, Kiro releases melodic house music as an artist himself. KIRO’s style could be described to draw influence from Galantis, Don Diablo, and Sigala. His first original tracks are ready to be released in 2017 and he has a number of great remixes under his belt.

KIRO has already performed for a big crowd at his home town Jyväskylä where he took the stage in front of 7000 people at one of the biggest student events at the local exhibition hall. More memorable performances are on their way as KIRO prepares himself for festival, clubs and various shows in Finland and abroad. His show is energetic and well excecuted combining visuals and fresh music in the mix.
